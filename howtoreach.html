Railways

The nearest Railway Station is Kharsaliya [KRSA] which is about 20 kms from Paroli. Bus, auto or private taxi can be availed from the railway station to reach Paroli Tirth. Express trains can be boarded from Godhra [GDA] and Vadodara [BRC] junctions
